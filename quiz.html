Global Issues — Food Insecurity

Chronic
Food
Insecurity

Long-term lack of reliable access to nutritious food caused by poverty, inequality, and weak food systems.

Global Issues — Food Insecurity

Acute
Food
Insecurity

Sudden severe food shortages caused by emergencies like conflict, disasters, or economic collapse.
